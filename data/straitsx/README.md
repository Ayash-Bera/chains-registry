# StraitsX

## Overview

StraitsX offers a payments infrastructure for digital assets, focusing on stablecoins like XUSD, XSGD, and XIDR. It enables seamless transactions between traditional finance and digital assets, with multi-chain support across Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain. The platform also provides services for both individuals and businesses, such as API solutions for payment and payout management.
